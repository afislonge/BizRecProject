RecBizPred

AI-Powered Restaurant Recommendation System

A machine learning model that recommends restaurants based on key factors like location, industry trends, customer sentiment, and competition, providing data-backed recommendations to users on the best place to visit.

SME sector accounts for over 90% of all businesses worldwide and contributes significantly to employment and GDP. However, research shows that around 20% of small businesses fail in their first year, and about 50% don’t survive beyond the fifth year​. According to a survey by CB Insights, the top reasons for business failure include poor market fit (42%) and running out of capital (29%)​. There's a growing demand for data-driven solutions that help entrepreneurs make more informed decisions about which businesses to pursue.

Our machine learning model offers personalized, real-time restaurants recommendations by analyzing market trends, customer sentiment, and competitive positioning, allowing entrepreneurs to make data-driven decisions with predictions ranked by success rates. It is highly scalable, adaptable to various sectors and geographies, enabling users to compare restaurants across industries and regions. With customizable filters for industry, location, and price, the model provides comprehensive insights based on financial metrics, consumer feedback, competitor analysis, and external market factors. The user-centric design ensures a clear, actionable interface suitable for both technical and non-technical entrepreneurs.
